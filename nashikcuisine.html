Gulachi poli

Gulachi Poli looks similar to an indian bread, but is actually a sweet dish.It is made from wheat flour, and it is filled with a sweet stuffing made out of gram flour, jaggery and peanuts.Gulachi Poli is typically made in the local households during festivals.

Bharli Vangi

Bharli Vangi is a famous side dish of this region, made to be eaten with Rotis, Parathas or even Pav bread. Small brinjals are stuffed with dry roasted crushed peanuts, which are spiced with chilli powder, coriander powder and oil. The dish is rich in nutrients and can be eaten for lunch or dinner.

Zunka Bhakar

Zunka Bhakri is, traditionally, a side dish paired with rotis. It is made out of gram flour, which acts as a base and vegetables like onion, mustard seeds and ginger garlic are also added for flavour.


Misal

Misal Pav is a signature dish of Western Maharashtra, especially Nasik. The dish consists of Pav bread, which is served with two gravies, one is a spicy curry and another chilli gravy called ‘Kutt.’ It is served with fried potato shreds. A very popular street food in Nasik!


Makai Chidwa

Makai Chivda is a light snack made from corn or maize. Corn flakes are given a tasty avatar with this recipe. You can have a dish as simple or complex as you need. Chilli powder, peanuts and curry leaves are the staple ingredients and it is served with tea.


Thalipeeth

A call for all the authentic Maharashtrian fans, their super delicious and healthy Thalipeeth is a dish to devour all at once. The savory multi-grain flatbread accompanied by the spicy achaar is the popular Indian pancake of Maharashtra.

Special Dishes

Danyachi Usal

It is a simple, sophisticated, and nutritious dish. Danyachi usal, prepared using cooked peanuts perked with traditional spices are a flavorsome, tongue-licking meal to gobble down as an evening snack.

Padval Dalimbya

A lip-smacking vegetable dish, padval dalimbya is a combination of sweet, sour, savor a brilliant array of flavor on the palette. The split snake gourd, field beans, and seasoning of oil complete the dish.

Jhatka Pani Puri

A chaat that welcomes you with all the heart, Jhatka pani puri is an extra spicy version of Indian’s adored street food. While it all comes with multiple spice levels, it’s difficult to pass it all.

Pineapple Sheera

A simple sheer variety of sheera, that’s obsessed by people for generations. The pineapple sheera is a sweet delight to binge on your good and bad days. With ample of dry fruits and a pinch of saffron, VITS provides you the best.
