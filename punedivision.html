Introduction of Pune !

Ensconed in the majestic Sahayadri mountain, the bustling city of Pune, second-largest in Maharashtra and the eight-largest metropolis in India, is hailed for it's impressive architecture, which is a legacy of the Marathas who were the erstwhile rulers. Dotted with grand forts, ancient caves, palatial structures(wadas) and temples, the city is steeped in spirituality and history.Pune is a treasure trove of delicacies.
