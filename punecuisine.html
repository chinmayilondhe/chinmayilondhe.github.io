Bedekar misal

In the bylanes of Narayan Peth,there is an iconic spot that serves mouth-watering misal.If you don't like your misal too spicy, here you have an option to choose milder gravy.
Where: 418 Munjabacha Bol, Narayan Peth When: 8am to 7 pm

Chitale Bhakarwadi

No one leaves Pune without trying the absolutely delish Bhakarvadis from Chitale Bandhu. No one!
Where: 759/54, Deccan Gymkhana, Opp Gharware Chowk
When: 9 am to 8:30 pm. Closed on Mondays.

JJ Garden Vada Pav

One of the oldest and most famous vada-pav stalls in town, this one is a must-try if you haven’t had it yet. Slightly expensive than your usual ones, but the size justifies the price. Don’t forget to ask for some churra on the side.
Where: 948, And 949, Bootee Street, Camp When: 9 am to 10 pm


Sujata Mastani

Pune’s blended ice cream and milkshake specialty will give any dessert a run for its money. Best enjoyed in summer, a trip to Sujata Mastani is a must if you’re in Pune.
Where: 1259, Sadashiv Peth, Near Nimbalkar Talim Chowk
When: 10:30am to 11:30pm


Masala Dosa

Vaishali is one of the most popular restaurant among the student community of Pune. Vaishali attracts all the college crowd. The ambience is youthful and price factor is also average.
Where: Fergusson College Rd, Rage Path, Ganeshwadi, Shivajinagar
When: 7am to 11pm


Sabudana Vada

Crispy on the outside, super soft and pillowy on the inside; this Sabudana Vada at JM Road is the bomb. Served with cool cucumber dahi and hotter thecha chutney. Where : Opposite Jangli Maharaj Temple, Next to Kensha Mobile Shop, JM Road When: 11 am to 9 pm

Special Dishes

Shrewsbury Biscuits

People from all around the country are crazy about the Shrewsbury Biscuits from Kayani Bakery. Where: 6, East Street, Camp Road When: 7:30 am to 1 pm and 3:30 to 8 pm

Bun Maska

If you’re around Dhole Patil Road, stop at Vouhman Cafe for a kadak cup of chai with some yummy bun maska. Where: Millennium Star, Near Ruby Hall, BS Dhole Patil Path When: 6 am to 6 pm

Kalyan Bhel

When on Law College Road, make a pit stop at Kalyan Bhel for some finger licking snack break and you won’t be disappointed. Try any chaat here and you’ll keep coming for more. Where: Next To Karur Vysya Bank, Mayur Colony Pedestrian Overpass, Mayur Colony, Kothrud When: 1 pm to 10.30 pm

Daal Baati

The absolute best place in Pune to gorge on this Rajasthani speciality. Maybe a little far from the city side, but the ride is totally worth it. Where: Plot no 67, Shree Vihar, Transport Nagar, Near Bhakti Shakti Garden, Nigdi When: 11 am to 11.30 pm

Bahubali Thali

We suggest you go hear with a big appetite and couple of friends to help you relish this giant thali that is super fresh and absolutely delicious. Where: Cat No.1206, B-19, J.m. Road, Opp.To Sambhaji Garden When: 11 am to 11.30 pm

Kheema Pav

This one is an oldie but goodie. Situated at F C Road, chances are you’ve already been to this one. Though we love all dishes here, nothing comes as close to the mouth-watering Kheema Pav. The Bun Omelette comes a close second. Where: Fergusson College Road, Deccan Gymkhana, Near Goodluck Chowk When: 7.30 am to 11.30 pm

Manasi Gola

Every soul in Pune loves this sweet treat that is made of a crushed ice cone in a glass and topped with flavored syrups (strawberry, fruit, cola, Mango, Pineapple, and more). Where:Dajisaheb Natu Rd, Natu Baag, Shukrawar Peth, Pune When:3pm to 6pm
