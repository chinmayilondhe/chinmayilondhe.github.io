Hotels


Come,stay and enjoy your day!

Novotel Pune

Featuring an open-air swimming pool, a fitness centre and a Kid’s play corner with Xbox 360, Kapla set and Play Table, Novotel Pune is located in Pune.

Hyatt Pune

Hyatt Pune is situated in the heart of the city, a 10-minute drive from Pune Airport. It offers luxurious rooms with ergonomic furnishings and large bathrooms. The staff and location was perfect.

The Orchid

Located 17 km from the historic Aga Khan Palace, The Orchid offers guests a 24-hour front desk along with a host of food and beverage options to choose from.

Radisson Blu Hotel

Located in Pune City, Radisson Blu Hotel Pune Kharadi offers spacious rooms with flat-screen TV. It features 3 dining options, an outdoor swimming pool, spa centre and free Wi-Fi.

Conrad Pune

Featuring 6 dining options, an outdoor temperature controlled swimming pool and 24- hour fitness centre, Conrad Pune - Luxury by Hilton offers accommodation in Pune.

Fort Jadhavgadh

Offering the facilities and benefits of an outdoor pool and spa and wellness centre, Fort Jadhavgadh, a heritage property, provides 24-hour front desk for the convenience of the guests.

Sheraton Grand Pune

Featuring old-world décor with contemporary comfort, Sheraton Grand Pune boasts 7 dining options, an outdoor pool, a fitness centre and a nightclub.

Vivanta Pune

Situated 2.1 km from Rajiv Gandhi Infotech Park in Hinjewadi, The Gateway Hotel Hinjawadi features a restaurant, bar and free WiFi throughout the property. The hotel has an outdoor pool and spa centre, and guests can enjoy a drink at the snack bar. Free private parking is available on site.

The Corinthians Resort & Club

Combining Greek and Egyptian designs, The Corinthians Resort & Club is a 15-minute drive from MG Road and Magarpatta City. It offers a full-service spa and several indoor and outdoor activities.
