Hotels


Come,stay and enjoy your day!

Radisson Blue hotel

Radisson Blu Hotel is located in Nagpur, only 10-minute drive away from Nagpur International Airport. outstanding services, cleaness and kindness.

Le Meridien Nagpur

Le Meridien Nagpur is 10-minute drive away from Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar International Airport and offers rooms with free WiFi. It has a 24-hour coffee shop and outdoor pool.

Tuli Veer Bagh resort

Located in Nagpur, Tuli Veer Bagh Resort offers 3-star accommodation with a garden. Among the facilities of this property are a restaurant, a 24-hour front desk and room service, along with free WiFi.

Tuli imperial

Tuli Imperial is set in the business hub of Nagpur. It houses a spacious landscaped pool and a fitness centre. Guests can also enjoy complimentary shuttle services to Nagpur International Airport.

Hotel Centre Point

The 3-star Hotel Centre Point is located in Nagpur, about 3 km from Nagpur Railway Station. Regional dishes are served at its on-site restaurant, while 24-hour room service is available.

Treebo Trend Hotel

Treebo Trend Hotel A1 is set in Nagpur, within 7 km of Vidarbha Cricket Association Stadium and 4.3 km of Deekshabhoomi.

Regenta Central Hotel

Set in Nagpur, 7 km from Vidarbha Cricket Association Stadium, Regenta Central Hotel & Convention Centre Nagpur offers accommodation with a restaurant, private parking, a fitness centre and a bar.

Urban Hermitage

Situated in Nagpur, 8 km from Vidarbha Cricket Association Stadium, Urban Hermitage features accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge.

Hotel Airport Centre Point

This hotel is situated just 500 metres from the Nagpur Airport, and a free airport pick-up service is provided.
