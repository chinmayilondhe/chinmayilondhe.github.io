Introduction of Konkan !

Konkan division is one of the six administrative divisions of Maharashtra state in India. It comprises the Konkan region, which occupies the entire west coast of Maharashtra. The two districts in the state capital Mumbai also fall in Konkan division.So koknkan is all about red soil, virgin beaches, and testy cuisine. smell of alphanso mangoes,and spicy sea food is real treasure of konkan ! one can never forget konkan, once visited.. so lets see how beautiful our konkan is !
