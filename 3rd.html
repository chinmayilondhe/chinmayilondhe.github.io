Maharashtra

Welcome to Maharashtra. A land whose sheer size and diversity will stun you. Enjoy Mountains that stretch out into the mists as far as the eye can see. The spirit of Maharashtra is cosmopolitan, forward-thinking, tolerant and vibrant. There is enough to keep connoisseurs of temples, forts, old monuments and art gainfully preoccupied here. Forts have played a vital role in the history of the state, given the rocky terrain of the Sahyadris. Each fort marks a military triumph, and each tells a story of strategy,warfare,intrigue and planning. Welcome aboard a travel experience that gives you a glimpse into this vibrant and beautiful land. Welcome to Maharashtra. A land untouched, unsullied, unlimited.
